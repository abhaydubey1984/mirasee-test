Expert in Your Topic, But Not in Online Course Building?

You're smart, so you already know that an online course is an ideal way to leverage your valuable specialist knowledge.

Whether you want to boost your professional credibility, grow your audience, increase your revenues or make a bigger impact on the world, creating your own online course will help you to accomplish those ambitions.

There's just one problem... When you don't have a lot of course-building experience, it's hard to figure out what you need to do, and how to make it all work.

You really don't have the time to spend endless months - maybe even years - figuring it out on your own.

And you don't want to risk investing all that time and effort into your course creation project, only to find that nobody's interested in buying your course after all! You want to get the course-building knowledge and skills you need NOW, so that you can make your course as successful (and profitable!) as possible.

The Course Builder's Bootcamp

My team and I have created this Bootcamp to get you up to speed with all the essentials of course creation and monetization, fast.

You'll learn all the steps and core ideas of my course building process, condensed for quick learning and application. And you'll learn what you need to do or change in your business, to become a successful online course creator.

You'll have access to the Bootcamp for 7 days to complete three video training modules. You'll have a short assignment to submit at the end of each module.

You'll be able to get help and support from our Course Building Strategists at any time, to get immediate, personalized advice

After a week, your access to the Bootcamp will be removed. We do this because there's compelling scientific evidence to suggest that having a real deadline will encourage you to work through the lessons consistently and productively.

  • What it really takes to become a successful (and profitable!) builder of online courses
  • The 4 big trends happening right now in online education, and how you can position yourself to best take advantage of them
  • How to identify topics based on your expert knowledge, that you could offer in an online course
  • The truth about marketing an online course - and the key opportunities for course creators in 2019
  • By the end of this lesson, you'll understand the massive opportunity of online courses and what you need to move forward to achieve your goals

  • How to determine your best course marketing strategy so that your ideal students are pleased to hear about it
  • The simple formula to establish yourself as an authority so that you can sell your course before you build it
  • Why it makes sense to sell your course before you build it, and why your paying students will gladly help you with the course creation process!
  • How to make sure the topic you choose is something students will pay you to teach them
  • By the end of this lesson, you'll understand what you need to change in your business and what topic to focus on to make your online course-building ambitions a reality

  • How to design and build your course quickly and painlessly, without content problems or technology issues
  • 4 Effortless Enrollment Strategies to put paying students into your course, fast
  • How to help your students get the best possible outcomes so that they not only complete your course, but spread the word and send new students your way
  • My Pilot Checklist: 10 Things You Need to Get Started (and 6 You Don't)
